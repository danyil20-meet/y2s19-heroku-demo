My Story

I was born in Ukraine in 2002. Since then I stayed at home till 2017 when I've finally passed exams and got to the NAALE Program. And..now I'm in Israel

If you don't know what is NAALE - I'm pretty sure you have no clue about the boarding school's food,about how is it possible to live in one room with 2 more dudes during the whole year and.. you definetely don't know how does it feel to find your second family.

It was kinda complicated to get used to the boarding school life. All of these new places, unknown people and weird rules. For example, we are not allowed to take a shower after the curfew time. But, you always get used to stuff. Besides that, after a short time I've started loving this place.

During my first year of high school I've dedided to apply to the program called MEET and..I PASSED. That's why this Website exists xD
